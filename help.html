In order to install classic Thingamajigs, you'll need Forge, specifically the one for each version cycle of the mod. The most up to date and popular for it's cycle is MC 1.20.1.


If installing Thingamajigs 2, Overpowered Mastery or CMATD, you'll need NeoForge. Usually this one updates to each major MC version (well almost). 1.21.1 is the most popular and well documented version. Hopefully newest and 1.21.1 versions will always be synced in terms of features. If not, be very patient, it may take months or longer to update (4+ different mod codebases to manage!).

Thingamajigs Long Roads or Thingamajigs Railroadways requires NeoForge and Thingamajigs 2, as it needs it as a base or 'core' mod to work. These mods always try to update just after the main mod updates, as they are basically feature complete.
